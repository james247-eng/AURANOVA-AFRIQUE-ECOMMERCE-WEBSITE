Our Story

Where African Heritage Meets Contemporary Luxury

AURANOVA-AFRIQUE

Born from a passion for African excellence and a vision to redefine luxury fashion.

AURANOVA-AFRIQUE emerged from the vibrant streets of Lagos, Nigeria, with a singular mission: to celebrate African craftsmanship while setting new standards in premium fashion. Our name combines "Aura" - the distinctive atmosphere of elegance, with "Nova" - representing new beginnings, and "Afrique" - our proud roots.

Every piece we create tells a story of heritage, passion, and meticulous attention to detail. From the finest fabrics to the skilled hands of our artisans, we ensure that every garment embodies the spirit of excellence that defines African luxury.
